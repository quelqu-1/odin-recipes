Ceviche

Description

Think of it like sushi salsa. A Mexican or Peruvian chef might scoff at this, but I think it's a helpful starting place for an American audience. Ceviche can include of a number of different ingredients (see our favorites below), but primarily consists of raw fish cured in citrus juice.

Ingredients

  • Red snapper
  • Lime juice
  • Serrano pepper
  • Red onion
  • more

Steps

  1. Cut fish into 1 cm cubes.
  2. Chop serrano, onion, cilantro, cucumber, and any other desired vegetables.
  3. Mix fish and vegetables in a bowl and cover with citrus juice.
  4. Let sit at least 30 minutes. Serve with tortilla chips or on tostadas.
